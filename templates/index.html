Car Damage Detection using CNN

Car Damage Detection:


Car damage detection using Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) is a cutting-edge application of deep learning in the automotive industry. CNNs are highly effective for this task due to their ability to extract intricate features from images. In this context, a CNN is trained to analyze images of vehicles and identify any signs of damage or defects. The process typically involves a large dataset of labeled images, with each image categorized as either "undamaged" or "damaged." The CNN learns to recognize patterns, textures, and shapes associated with various types of car damage, such as dents, scratches, or broken parts. Once trained, the model can accurately classify unseen car images, providing a valuable tool for automakers, insurance companies, and vehicle inspectors. Car damage detection using CNNs enhances the efficiency and accuracy of damage assessment, streamlining processes like insurance claims processing and vehicle maintenance, ultimately contributing to improved safety and customer satisfaction in the automotive industry.
